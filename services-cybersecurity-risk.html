Cybersecurity as a Service

We help small and medium-sized businesses integrate essential cybersecurity capabilities without building a large in-house team, while supporting large enterprise and government clients with dedicated, specialist coverage.

24/7 Security Operations Center (SOC)

Continuous monitoring, incident response and SIEM expertise to detect threats in real time.

Threat Intelligence & Hunting

Dark web monitoring, IDS/IPS integration and proactive threat detection before damage occurs.

Vulnerability Assessment & Penetration Testing (VAPT)

Regular security patching and penetration testing to safeguard IT infrastructure, including data centres and web portals.

Identity & Access Management

Backup & Disaster Recovery

Resilient backup architecture and recovery planning so operations continue through disruption.

Risk Assessment & Management

Risk management is crucial for any policy decision-making process, business strategy, or mission-driven program. We tailor our approach to the type of organization we’re working with.

For Government Clients

Policy risk identification, impact-focused assessment, and strategic mitigation planning that safeguard public interest and institutional integrity.

For Corporate Clients

Operational and regulatory risk identification, quantitative and qualitative assessment, and agile mitigation strategies embedded into enterprise risk management (ERM).

For Nonprofit Organizations

Mission-critical risk identification and contextual assessment that protects programs, donors and community relationships.
