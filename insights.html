SIEM 101

Think of SIEM like a security camera system for your entire digital world — servers, apps, emails and cloud accounts. It collects clues like login attempts and firewall blocks, analyzes them around the clock, and alerts your team the moment something looks off, often catching intrusions before customers ever notice.

Phishing Scams Unmasked

Phishing remains one of the most common ways attackers get a foothold, tricking people into handing over credentials or clicking malicious links. Recognizing the warning signs and building a culture of healthy skepticism is one of the highest-leverage defenses an organization can build.

AI and Cybersecurity: Friend or Foe?

Artificial intelligence is reshaping cybersecurity on both sides of the fight — powering faster threat detection and response, while also giving attackers new tools for more convincing scams. The organizations that win are the ones that adopt AI deliberately, with the right guardrails in place.
